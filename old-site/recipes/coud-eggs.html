Make the "Cloud Eggs"

By Henry Phillips on Henry's Kitchen

Link to source video here. Highly recommended watch!

Description

Today we're gonna be making "cloud eggs". Eggs are very high in cholesterol. 2-4 servings, be ready to share! You better not be hogging these all up for yourself asshole!

Ingredients

  • Six eggs
  • Salt
  • Ground pepper
  • Grated parm
  • Broccoli left over from a previous failed dish
  • Little Toasts (Bruschetta)

Steps

  1. Break eggs
  2. Seperate yolk from the rest of the egg in seperate bowls
  3. Use the tossing method -- toss the yolk between the two halves of the egg shell
  4. Take the egg white bowls and bour the egg whites into the seventh workbowl
  5. Tossing!
  6. Add salt, ground pepper, grated parm, to the workbowl
  7. Veggies!
  8. Whip it up! You want that thick bouncy consistency kind of like your mom
    would make if she was making maringue pie when you were a kid.
  9. Pour Our Cloud Mixture onto a greased foil-lined baking sheet so we can put it into the oven
  10. Pour into four distinct "clouds" our "egg mounds
  11. Put the yolks in the oven for 2 minutes
  12. Reminisce about old memories and lost friends when waiting
  13. Put on Little Toasts (Bruschetta)
