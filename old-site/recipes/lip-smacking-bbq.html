How to Make Henry's Lip-Smacking BBQ Beer Fish

By Henry Phillips on Henry's Kitchen

Link to source video here. Highly recommended watch!

Description

Today we're gonna be making Henry's Lip-Smacking BBQ Beer Fish. Grilling fish on the gril requires a very special technique.
Fun fact: According to Henry, wild seal ejaculate was historically used by eskimos to grant special powers. (NOT FACT CHECKED)

Ingredients

  • Fresh Chives
  • Rosemary Sprigs
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Beer
  • Fish

Steps

  1. Cut Chives
  2. Cut Rosemary Sprigs
  3. Mince Onion
  4. Mince Garlic
  5. (TW: kinda nasty) Take all that shit, and stuff it into our fish. Way in there.
  6. Crack open a cold one and pour it all over our fish
  7. Make room on the grill for the fish
  8. Grill for 10 min. Check it halfway through and flip around.
  9. Mingle in the meantime
  10. Enjoy!
  11. Don't forget salt!!!!!

Remember: cooking is never easy.
